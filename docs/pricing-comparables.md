# Directory advertising pricing comparables

Sursă: web research, 14 mai 2026.
Scop: ancoră pentru pricing pe `/publicitate` când va fi cazul. Cifrele NU sunt comparabile direct cu noi — au scale diferit (G2/Capterra ~milioane visits/lună vs noi ~2.4k), audience diferit (B2B SaaS US vs B2B PV RO), value prop diferit (review platform vs verified directory). Folosește ca reference, nu ca formulă.

---

## G2.com

- **Niche:** B2B software / SaaS review directory (US-anchored, global)
- **Pricing model:** Hybrid — subscription plans pentru profil + add-on PPC (G2 Clicks) + Paid Promotions cu inventory cap
- **Public pricing:**
  - Free plan: $0 — [sell.g2.com/plans](https://sell.g2.com/plans)
  - Starter: $299/lună (sau $2.999/an, 16% off year 1; year 2 urcă la $599/lună) — [sell.g2.com/plans](https://sell.g2.com/plans)
  - Professional: sales-gated — [sell.g2.com/plans](https://sell.g2.com/plans)
  - Enterprise: sales-gated — [sell.g2.com/plans](https://sell.g2.com/plans)
  - G2 Clicks (PPC add-on): CPC variabil pe regiune și topic, nu publicat — [documentation.g2.com/docs/g2-clicks](https://documentation.g2.com/docs/g2-clicks)
  - Paid Promotions: inventory cap 3 produse per pagină, run 3 luni default, preț nu publicat — [documentation.g2.com/docs/paid-promotions](https://documentation.g2.com/docs/paid-promotions)
- **Estimated traffic:** ~3.1M visits/lună per SimilarWeb — [similarweb.com/website/g2.com](https://www.similarweb.com/website/g2.com/)
- **Notes:** Paid Promotions are inventory cap explicit (3 sloturi/categorie, 33% share-of-voice). Year-2 price jump 2x e relevant pentru tine — anchor pricing low e legit, dar setează renewal narrative din start.
- **Source date:** Plans page accesat 14 mai 2026

## Capterra (Gartner-owned)

- **Niche:** B2B software directory, model PPC + qualified leads (advisory)
- **Pricing model:** Pay-per-click cu minim $500/lună spend; bids start $2/click, increments $0.25
- **Public pricing:**
  - Listare profil: free — [capterra.com/legal/ppc-service-description](https://www.capterra.com/legal/ppc-service-description/)
  - PPC: $2 bid minim, minim $500/lună spend; CPC efectiv $5-$25+ în categorii competitive — [capterra.com/legal/ppc-service-description](https://www.capterra.com/legal/ppc-service-description/) și raportări vendor pe [retaintrust.com/advertising-on-capterra-and-software-advice](https://retaintrust.com/advertising-on-capterra-and-software-advice/)
  - Qualified Leads (advisory): pricing nu publicat — sales-gated
- **Estimated traffic:** ~2.4M visits/lună menționat pe SimilarWeb cross-reference G2 — [similarweb.com/website/g2.com/vs/capterra.com](https://www.similarweb.com/website/g2.com/vs/capterra.com/)
- **Notes:** Minim de $500/lună e un floor explicit pe care îl poți cita. Bids $2 minim arată că platforme mari pun praguri psihologice scăzute la entry-level.
- **Source date:** PPC service description (legal page) — actualizat cel mai recent vizibil 2024-2025

## Houzz Pro

- **Pricing model:** Subscription tier + advertising add-on geographic
- **Public pricing:**
  - Pro plan: nu listat numeric pe pagina oficială curentă (sales-gated); raportări terțe la $149/lună (Essential) și $249/lună (Pro) — [houzz.com/houzz-pro/pricing](https://www.houzz.com/houzz-pro/pricing) (oficial: doar tier names + free trial 30d)
  - Advertising Package: **"Starting at $499/mo"** — [houzz.com/houzz-pro/pricing](https://www.houzz.com/houzz-pro/pricing)
  - Additional user pe Pro: $60/user/lună — [houzz.com/houzz-pro/pricing](https://www.houzz.com/houzz-pro/pricing)
  - 12-month contract minim — [houzz.com/houzz-pro/pricing](https://www.houzz.com/houzz-pro/pricing)
- **Estimated traffic:** Not public la rezoluție concretă pe SimilarWeb gratis
- **Notes:** Advertising e separat de subscription. $499 e price-anchor pentru audiență local-service, US — relevant ca punct de comparație mental, NU ca target RO. 12-month lock e diferențiator: noi nu cerem asta.
- **Source date:** Pricing page accesat 14 mai 2026

## Yelp

- **Niche:** Local business advertising (US-anchored, multi-categorie)
- **Pricing model:** Subscription + CPC ads
- **Public pricing:**
  - Yelp Business Page: free — [business.yelp.com/local-business-pricing](https://business.yelp.com/local-business-pricing/)
  - Yelp Ads: **"From $150/lună"** — [business.yelp.com/local-business-pricing](https://business.yelp.com/local-business-pricing/)
  - Upgrade Package: **$180/lună** — [business.yelp.com/local-business-pricing](https://business.yelp.com/local-business-pricing/)
  - Ads + Upgrade combinat: **"From $270/lună"** — [business.yelp.com/local-business-pricing](https://business.yelp.com/local-business-pricing/)
  - Real-quote average pe `/costs/advertising`: typical $1.017/lună (n=812 quotes), range $660–$1.700 — [yelp.com/costs/advertising](https://www.yelp.com/costs/advertising) (last updated May 2026)
- **Estimated traffic:** Not public la rezoluție concretă în search results
- **Notes:** $150 entry-level e cel mai folositor anchor — local SMB advertising floor pe o platformă majoră. Dar real-quote average ($1k) arată că majoritatea cheltuiesc >> floor. Cancel anytime — fără lock.
- **Source date:** Local business pricing page și Cost of Advertising page, last updated May 2026

  - Plus: **€149/lună per domain** (billed annually) — [business.trustpilot.com/pricing](https://business.trustpilot.com/pricing)
  - Premium: **€369/lună per domain** (billed annually) — [business.trustpilot.com/pricing](https://business.trustpilot.com/pricing)
  - Enterprise: sales-gated — [business.trustpilot.com/pricing](https://business.trustpilot.com/pricing)
  - 12-month commitment pe toate paid — [business.trustpilot.com/pricing](https://business.trustpilot.com/pricing)
- **Estimated traffic:** Not public în search results
- **Notes:** Per-domain pricing e relevant — analog cu "per județ" sau "per oraș" pe la noi dacă ar fi cazul. €79 Starter cu gating la "new customers + sub $5M revenue" arată price-discrimination explicit.

## Observații generale (factuale, nu speculative)

- **Free tier e norma.** G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, Yelp, Bark, Afacerist — toate oferă listare gratuită cu features reduse. Paywall e doar pe visibility boost, advanced features, sau leads.
- **Sales-gating la tier-uri mari e standard.** G2 Professional/Enterprise, Houzz Pro, Trustpilot Enterprise, Capterra Qualified Leads — nicio platformă din astea nu listează prețul peste un anumit nivel.
- **Pricing model split:** SaaS B2B (G2, Capterra) merge spre PPC/PPL. Local services (Yelp, Houzz, Bark) merge spre subscription + PPC hybrid sau pure pay-per-lead. Review platforms (Trustpilot) sunt pure subscription.
- **Annual commitment e comun:** Trustpilot și Houzz Pro cer 12-month lock. Yelp și Bark sunt cancel-anytime. G2 Starter discount-ează annual cu 16%.
- **Per-domain / per-geo pricing:** Trustpilot vinde per-domain, Houzz Pro per-geo. Multiplicator natural — relevant dacă vrem vreodată "per județ" sau "național" tier (avem deja cu "Național Plus" 299€).
- **Floor entry-level la directorii relevante local-service US:** $99-$150/lună (Trustpilot Starter, Yelp Ads). RO general-purpose: ~10€/lună (Afacerist GOLD). Niciuna NU e direct comparabilă — diferă traffic, audience, value.
